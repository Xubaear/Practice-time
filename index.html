A Blog About Bangladeshi Music Bands


Bangladesh has a rich musical heritage, with bands that have made significant contributions to the music scene.These bands have played a crucial role in shaping the music culture in Bangladesh, blending traditional sounds with modern influences. Here are some notable bands:

  • Warfaze
  • Artcell
  • Shironamhin
  • Aurthohin
  • Ashes

Warfaze:

Warfaze is one of the pioneering heavy metal bands in Bangladesh, formed in 1984. They are known for their powerful music and meaningful lyrics. Their top 5 best songs are:

  1. Purnota
  2. Rupkotha
  3. Maya
  4. Asha
  5. Obak Valobasha
